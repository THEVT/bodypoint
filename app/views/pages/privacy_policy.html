Medical Privacy

The privacy of your medical information is ensured via HIPPA and its Privacy Rule. The Privacy Rule provides federal protections for personal health information held by covered entities and grants patients an array of rights with respect to that information. At the same time, the Privacy Rule allows for the disclosure of personal health information needed for patient care and other purposes. You may wish to download and review the document entitled, “Your Health Information Privacy Rights” from the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services at www.hhs.gov to gain a better understanding of medical privacy and your rights.
